Micro-G Update

November 13th, 2015

"We have submitted our preliminary design to NASA. The final design is an assembly of ~9 parts: the cone, shaft, 5 plates, piston, and blocking bar. The cone piece that is the first part to make direct contact with the surface is threaded for easy of penetration. The shaft serves as the housing for the piston and the plates attached by individual hinges. A person pushing on the piston activates the plate assembly and they slide out of their holes and grip the m_barnes. Our design underwent many changes during the brainstorming procedure. Our inspirations were river anchors. screwpile drills, and the fractal design used on the Rosetta comet." - Katrina; Co-Head of Micro-g Team
