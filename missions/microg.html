In the past, challenges have been based on problems astronauts may face on the International Space Station, during spacewalks, and on the moon during the upcoming Artemis Missions. Throughout the academic year, students work alongside NASA engineers through the full iterative design process, including the creation and delivery of several proposals, design reviews, academic papers, and presentations.
