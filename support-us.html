Thank you so much for your interest in supporting CSI! Your donation will go directly to purchasing technical supplies, funding conference and competition travel, hosting career development events, and to other causes that directly impact students and student life. We accept contributions from both individuals and corporate sponsors (including gifts and in-kind donations for our 13 different missions). To learn more about our work, we invite you to explore our website, or to hear from our students directly about the impact that CSI has had on them in the video below.

Make a Donation to CSI

To support the work we do, visit Columbia's Pi Day donation page, click “give now” and choose Engineering Student Club Initiative in the drop-down menu. Once you have entered your personal information, be sure to include Columbia Space Initiative in the special instructions box. Thank you and ad astra!

Corporate Sponsorships

Corporate sponsors help provide project and travel funding, hardware and software, and technical expertise to our 13+ ongoing student projects. To learn more about what it means to support CSI, feel free to browse through our sponsorship packet below. If you’re interested in becoming a corporate sponsor, please reach out to us at columbiaspaceinitiative@gmail.com!
